## Art Direction Notes

- Avoided in-image text and logos so the websites can own typography, CTAs, and brand language.
- Used realistic commercial photography rather than glossy UI renders.
- Chose booking/payment/quote/reporting moments that can support conversion sections.
- Kept images premium but grounded, with natural light and operational context.
